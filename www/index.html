FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions

How to get registered in Loyalty Program

Go to Paytm , Search for Virgo Gold Club , Enter Your Number to Get Registered.

Who Can Use this App

This app is only for Contractors and Carpenters.

How to Change the language

Go to the login screen and from there you will find the options to select the language as per your convenience.

How to Scan the QR Code

Open Mobile App, click on Scan Virgo QR at the bottom & Scan the Code.

Where we can find the QR Code

You can find the QR Code at the top most part of the sheet.

When We can redeem the points

You can redeem the points whenever you want.

When I Will get the payment

After your request , it will go for the approval once approval done payment will be tranferred.

How to Use the App

You will find the app video in the What's section.

For Support Where do I need to Contact

You have to call us on 9817922222.

My QR Code is not getting Scanned

Please tell us the QR Code Number which is written in the sticker or please click image and Whatsapp us on 9817922222.

When I am Scanning the QR Code , it shows the Duplicate Scan what to do

Please visit the Dealer from where you purchased the Sheet , he will give you the solution.

What is the Point Value

For every point you will get 0.70 Rs.

Where I can see my Scanned QR Codes

Please go to the bottom section on the app and click on Transaction.

From Where I can redeem my points

Please go to the bottom section on the app and click on Redeem or Please click on the redeem button given in membership card.
